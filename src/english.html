I teach one-on-one English lessons online to native speakers of Mandarin. You can contact me via WeChat or Line if you are interested.

Methodology

Learning a language takes a lot more than just weekly lessons with a tutor. If you want to make real progress, you have to spend lots of time studying on your own. Durring our lessons, I'll help you setup an effective and efficient study routine that makes use of modern language learning technologies.

Once we have established an effective way for you to study, our lessons will remain mostly conversational. Put simply, you should be learning new words during your independent study time, and during lessons I will help you practice applying thoes words in real conversation.
